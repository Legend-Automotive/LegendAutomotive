THE CHRONICLE

2014

The Spark

Founded in a small workshop in Stuttgart, Legend Automotive began as a boutique engineering firm specializing in high-voltage racing batteries. Our founders saw the untapped potential of electric torque in the luxury segment.

2019

Breaking the Barrier

The launch of the 'Sovereign One' shattered world records for production electric vehicles. It was the first time luxury and electric range crossed the 500-mile threshold without sacrificing weight or aesthetics.
